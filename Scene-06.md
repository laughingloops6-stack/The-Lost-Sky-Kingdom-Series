# Scene Title

The Trial of Wisdom

---

# Story Continuity

This scene begins immediately after the First Crystal Trial.

The first frame must exactly match the last frame of Scene 05.

---

# Scene Description

The second crystal door opens slowly.

A circular chamber filled with floating crystal orbs appears.

Each orb glows with a different color.

At the center of the chamber stands an ancient crystal pedestal.

As Arin, Bruno, and Byte step inside, the entrance seals shut behind them.

The Crystal Guardian's voice echoes through the chamber.

**CRYSTAL GUARDIAN**

*"Strength alone cannot unlock the Sky Kingdom..."*

*"Wisdom reveals the true path."*

Suddenly, glowing symbols appear across the floor.

The floating crystal orbs begin moving in a mysterious pattern.

Byte scans the chamber.

Ancient symbols start translating one by one.

**BYTE**

*"These symbols describe the history of the Sky Kingdom..."*

*"We must activate the crystal orbs in the correct order."*

Arin studies the glowing walls.

He notices that the murals match the order shown on the pedestal.

He carefully touches the first blue orb.

Then the golden orb.

Finally, the white crystal orb.

The chamber trembles.

For a tense moment...

Nothing happens.

Then...

The pedestal shines with brilliant blue light.

The sealed exit slowly opens.

The heroes have solved the Trial of Wisdom.

---

# Ending Frame

A beam of blue light shoots upward from the crystal pedestal.

Far ahead...

The third and final crystal door slowly begins to open.

Fade to Scene 07.
