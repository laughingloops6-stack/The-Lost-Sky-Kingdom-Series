# Thumbnail Prompts

## Purpose

This folder contains all official AI thumbnail prompts for **The Lost Sky Kingdom** series.

Every thumbnail must attract viewers while maintaining perfect story continuity.

---

## Thumbnail Style

- Disney Pixar Quality
- Ultra Cinematic
- Bright Colors
- High Contrast
- Emotional Expressions
- Magical Lighting
- Family Friendly
- 4K Quality

---

## Thumbnail Rules

- One clear main subject
- Show the episode's biggest mystery
- No spoilers
- Clean composition
- Large empty space for title text
- Strong focus on the hero
- Consistent character appearance

---

## Prompt Structure

Episode Number

Main Subject

Background

Lighting

Camera Angle

Emotion

Color Palette

Art Style

Quality Details

Negative Prompt

---

## Text Placement

Leave 25–30% empty space for the YouTube title.

Do not place important characters in that area.

---

## Negative Prompt

- No blurry image
- No duplicate characters
- No extra limbs
- No watermark
- No text
- No logo
- No low quality
- No cropped faces
- No horror elements
