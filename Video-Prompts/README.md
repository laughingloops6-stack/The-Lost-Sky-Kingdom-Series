# Video Prompts

## Purpose

This folder contains all AI video generation prompts for **The Lost Sky Kingdom** series.

Every video prompt must follow the official Story Bible, World Bible, and Character Bible to maintain perfect continuity across all episodes.

---

## Standard Prompt Structure

Every prompt must include:

- Episode Number
- Scene Number
- Story Context
- Attached Reference Image
- Character Lock
- Camera Lock
- Environment Lock
- Animation Instructions
- Lighting
- Ending Frame
- Negative Prompt

---

## Video Style

- Disney Pixar Quality
- 3D Animation
- Ultra Cinematic
- 4K HDR
- Smooth Camera Movement
- Volumetric Lighting
- Family Friendly
- High Detail
- Consistent Characters

---

## Camera Rules

- Smooth cinematic movement only
- No camera shake
- No sudden cuts
- Match previous scene's last frame
- End each scene with a frame ready for the next scene

---

## Character Rules

- Maintain exact appearance
- No redesign
- No duplicate characters
- Same clothing throughout the episode
- Same personality and behavior

---

## Scene Duration

- 10 Seconds per scene
- Around 18 scenes per episode
- Approximate episode duration: 3 minutes

---

## Status

Official video prompt library for Season 1.
