# Story Bible

## Title
The Lost Sky Kingdom

## Genre
Fantasy • Adventure • Mystery • Action

## Target Audience
13+ Teens and Young Adults

## Story Summary

Thousands of years ago, a powerful kingdom floated above the clouds. It disappeared after the Sky Crystal was shattered.

In the present day, an ordinary boy discovers a mysterious map that leads to the forgotten Sky Kingdom. Along with his friends, he begins a dangerous journey to uncover the truth, restore the kingdom, and stop an ancient darkness from returning.

## Main Themes

- Friendship
- Courage
- Destiny
- Sacrifice
- Hope

## Seasons

- Season 1 – Discovery of the Sky Kingdom
- Season 2 – Rise of the Dark Empire
- Season 3 – The Final Sky War
