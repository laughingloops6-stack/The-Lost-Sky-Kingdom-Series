# Story Summary

After unlocking the Crystal Gate in Episode 3, Foxy, Bruno, and Byte enter the ancient Crystal Corridor for the first time.

Inside, they discover an enormous forgotten structure built by the Lost Sky Kingdom civilization.

The Sky Crystal Key reacts to the corridor and begins guiding them deeper inside.

As they solve magical crystal puzzles and cross dangerous pathways, they finally reach the Guardian Chamber.

A gigantic Crystal Guardian awakens.

Instead of attacking, the Guardian tests the heroes' wisdom, courage, and teamwork.

After successfully completing the three Crystal Trials, the Guardian accepts them as worthy explorers.

The Guardian reveals the first breathtaking view of the Lost Sky Kingdom and warns the heroes that a mysterious Crystal Corruption has begun spreading across the kingdom.

The Guardian opens a magical portal leading toward the next stage of their adventure.

The episode ends with Foxy, Bruno, and Byte standing before the glowing portal, ready to continue their journey.
