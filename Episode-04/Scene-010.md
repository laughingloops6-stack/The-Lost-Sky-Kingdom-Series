# Scene Description

The glowing crystal path leads the heroes to an enormous palace floating above the clouds.

The palace gates slowly open by themselves.

Ancient crystal statues stand silently on both sides.

The Sky Crystal Key shines with intense blue light.

Arin steps forward.

The moment he crosses the palace entrance...

Thousands of crystal lights illuminate the entire hall.

Massive crystal chandeliers sparkle overhead.

Ancient murals begin glowing across the walls.

They tell the story of the Lost Sky Kingdom...

Its rise...

Its golden age...

And the beginning of a terrible war.

Bruno stares at the murals in amazement.

Byte records every detail.

Suddenly...

One mural changes.

It shows a mysterious warrior wearing a crystal crown.

The warrior looks exactly like Arin.

The three heroes freeze.

Arin quietly whispers...

*"How is this possible...?"*
