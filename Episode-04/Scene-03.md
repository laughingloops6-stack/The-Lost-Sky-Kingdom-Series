# Episode 04 – Scene 03
## Title: Eyes in the Darkness

### Location
Ancient Crystal Chamber – Hidden Beneath the Sky Palace

### Time
Night

---

## Scene Description

The silence is broken by a deep metallic sound.

CLANG...

CLANG...

The hidden chamber slowly awakens.

Dust falls from the ceiling as ancient crystal pillars begin glowing one after another.

Arin cautiously steps inside.

His breathing echoes through the enormous chamber.

At the center stands a massive circular platform covered in forgotten symbols.

The blue light suddenly changes...

...to crimson.

The temperature drops instantly.

A low growl fills the darkness.

Two glowing red eyes appear once again.

This time...

they do not disappear.

A tall shadow slowly walks forward.

Its body is covered in cracked black crystal armor.

Its face remains hidden beneath a broken mask.
