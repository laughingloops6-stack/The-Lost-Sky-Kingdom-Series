# Episode 04 – Scene 02
## Title: The Whispering Corridor

### Location
The Inner Crystal Corridor – Sky Palace

### Time
Night

---

## Scene Description

The corridor is silent.

Blue crystal walls glow faintly, casting long shadows across the polished floor.

A cold wind moves through the empty hallway...

...but there are no windows.

A strange whisper echoes.

Not one voice.

Hundreds.

Ancient.

Forgotten.

Arin slowly walks forward.

Every step creates a soft echo.

His hand instinctively reaches toward the glowing crystal wall.

The moment his fingers touch it—

FLASH!

A vision appears.

The Sky Kingdom...

before it was destroyed.

Children laughing.

Flying guardians protecting the skies.

Massive crystal towers shining like stars.

Then...

Dark clouds swallow the sky.

A terrifying black energy erupts from the horizon.

The laughter disappears.

Everything burns.

The vision ends instantly.

Arin falls to one knee, breathing heavily.

A mysterious voice whispers directly into his mind.

> "The past never truly dies..."

> "It waits..."

> "...for someone worthy to remember."

Arin slowly stands.

His eyes now glow brighter than before.

Somewhere in the darkness...

Two glowing red eyes silently watch him.

CUT TO BLACK.
