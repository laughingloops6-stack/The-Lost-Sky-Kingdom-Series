# Episode 04 – Scene 05

## Title
The First Crystal Trial

### Location
Hall of Courage

### Time
Night

---

## Scene Description

The left crystal door opens with a deep rumble.

A narrow bridge of glowing crystal stretches across a bottomless abyss.

Blue mist hides everything below.

The Crystal Guardian's voice echoes through the chamber.

**CRYSTAL GUARDIAN**

*"Only those who trust their courage may cross the Hall of Courage."*

Arin takes a deep breath and steps onto the bridge.

The moment his foot touches the crystal...

The bridge begins to crack.

Large crystal fragments fall into the darkness.

Bruno hesitates.

Byte quickly scans the bridge and discovers a pattern.

Small glowing crystal symbols appear for a few seconds before fading.

**BYTE**

*"Only the glowing crystals are safe! Follow the light!"*

Arin carefully jumps from one glowing crystal tile to another.

Bruno follows with determination.

Suddenly...

One crystal tile collapses beneath Bruno's feet.

He loses his balance.

Arin instantly grabs Bruno's hand.

For a moment...

Both heroes hang over the endless abyss.

Byte activates a burst of blue energy, creating a temporary crystal platform beneath them.

Together, they climb back onto the bridge.

The final glowing crystal lights up.

The three friends leap forward at the same time.

They land safely on the other side.

The bridge behind them disappears into sparkling blue light.

The Crystal Guardian watches silently.

A faint smile appears on its ancient face.

---

## Ending Frame

A massive crystal emblem rises from the floor.

It glows with brilliant blue light, signaling that the First Trial has been completed.

The second crystal door slowly begins to open.

Fade to Scene 06.
