# Location

The Hall of Unity

---

# Scene Description

The third crystal door opens with a powerful rumble.

A magnificent circular chamber appears before the heroes.

At its center floats a giant blue crystal surrounded by three glowing platforms.

As Arin, Bruno, and Byte step inside...

the chamber doors slam shut.

The Crystal Guardian appears once again.

Its glowing eyes scan each hero.

**CRYSTAL GUARDIAN**

*"The final trial cannot be completed alone."*

*"Only those who trust one another shall move forward."*

Three beams of blue light shoot from the giant crystal.

Each beam locks onto one hero.

The platforms begin moving apart.

Arin...

Bruno...

and Byte are separated.

The crystal starts losing its glow.

Byte quickly realizes what must be done.

**BYTE**

*"We must activate our platforms at exactly the same moment!"*

The three heroes nod.

They count together...

**Three...**

**Two...**

**One...**

All three place their hands on the glowing crystal symbols simultaneously.

A brilliant explosion of blue light fills the chamber.

The giant crystal awakens.

Golden energy spreads across every wall.

The Guardian watches silently...

Then slowly lowers its head in respect.

---

# Ending Frame

The massive crystal at the center shines brighter than ever.

A hidden gateway begins rising from the floor.

Beyond it...

the first glimpse of the Lost Sky Kingdom becomes visible.

Fade to Scene 08.
