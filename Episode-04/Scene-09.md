# Scene Title

The Silent Watcher

---

# Story Continuity

This scene begins immediately after Scene 08.

The first frame must exactly match the last frame of Scene 08.

No time skip.

No character redesign.

---

# Location

The Entrance Plaza of the Lost Sky Kingdom

---

# Scene Description

The ancient gateway closes silently behind the heroes.

Arin, Bruno, and Byte carefully step into the abandoned Sky Kingdom.

Every building is covered with glowing crystal vines.

The city is silent...

Almost too silent.

Suddenly...

A faint sound echoes across the empty streets.

Footsteps.

Not theirs.

The three heroes immediately stop.

Byte scans the area.

**BYTE**

*"Movement detected..."*

The camera slowly pans toward the top of a distant crystal tower.

A hooded figure stands there, completely still.

Its silver cloak moves gently in the wind.

Its face cannot be seen.

The mysterious figure watches the heroes for only a moment...

...then vanishes into the light.

Arin takes one cautious step forward.

**ARIN**

*"Someone else is here..."*

The Sky Crystal Key glows once again.

As it glows...

Ancient crystal symbols begin lighting up across the city streets.

A forgotten path slowly reveals itself.

The heroes exchange determined looks.

Without another word...

they follow the glowing path deeper into the Lost Sky Kingdom.

---

# Camera Direction

Begin with a wide establishing shot of the abandoned city.

Slow tracking shot following the heroes.

Cut to a high-angle shot of the hooded figure on the tower.

Finish with a slow push toward the glowing crystal path.
