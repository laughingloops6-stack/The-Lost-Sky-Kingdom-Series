# Scene Title

The Ancient Vision

---

# Location

The Grand Crystal Hall

---

# Scene Description

The glowing mural suddenly comes alive.

The Crystal-Crowned Warrior slowly steps out of the wall as a shimmering projection made of blue crystal light.

The entire hall falls silent.

Arin, Bruno, and Byte stare in disbelief.

The ancient warrior raises one hand.

Without speaking...

A wave of crystal energy surrounds Arin.

The palace disappears.

Arin finds himself standing in the Sky Kingdom as it existed thousands of years ago.

The city is alive.

People smile.

Flying guardians patrol the skies.

Crystal dragons soar above the floating islands.

Then...

The peaceful sky suddenly turns dark.

A massive black storm forms above the kingdom.

Dark crystal creatures attack from every direction.

The ancient warrior draws a glowing crystal sword and leads the kingdom's defenders into battle.

The vision ends in a blinding flash.

Arin opens his eyes and falls to one knee.

His heart is racing.

The ancient projection looks at him.

For the first time, it speaks.

**CRYSTAL WARRIOR**

*"The past has chosen you..."*

*"The future now depends on your courage."*

The projection slowly fades away.
