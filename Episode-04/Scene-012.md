# Scene Description

The glowing crystal map on the palace floor begins rotating slowly.

Ancient gears hidden beneath the palace come to life.

The entire chamber trembles.

A massive circular portal rises from the ground.

Its surface shimmers with brilliant blue and golden energy.

Arin, Bruno, and Byte stare at it in complete silence.

The Sky Crystal Key floats into the air.

A beam of light connects the Key to the portal.

Suddenly...

A mysterious voice echoes through the chamber.

**MYSTERIOUS VOICE**

*"The first trial is complete..."*

*"But the true journey begins now."*

The portal opens completely.

For a brief moment...

A dark shadow with glowing crimson eyes appears inside the portal.

It silently watches the heroes...

...then disappears.

The portal remains open.

Arin steps forward with determination.

Bruno smiles confidently.

Byte activates his scanner.

The three friends walk toward the glowing portal.

Just before they enter...

The screen fades to black.
