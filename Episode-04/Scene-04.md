# Episode 04 – Scene 04

## Title
The Crystal Guardian Awakens

### Location
Guardian Chamber

### Time
Night

---

## Scene Description

The mysterious shadow slowly fades away.

For a brief moment...

Everything becomes completely silent.

Suddenly—

The enormous crystal floor begins to tremble.

Deep cracks spread across the chamber, glowing with brilliant blue energy.

Massive crystal pillars rise from the ground.

A deafening roar echoes throughout the chamber.

The giant Crystal Guardian slowly emerges from beneath the ancient platform.

Standing over twenty feet tall, the Guardian's body is forged from glowing blue crystal and ancient golden armor.

Its eyes shine like twin stars.

The Guardian looks directly at Arin.

Foxy, Bruno, and Byte instinctively step back in fear.

The Sky Crystal Key begins glowing brighter than ever before.

The Guardian raises its enormous hand...

...but instead of attacking...

it speaks.

**CRYSTAL GUARDIAN**

*"Only those with a pure heart may walk the path of the Sky Kingdom."*

The chamber falls silent.

A glowing crystal symbol appears beneath the heroes' feet.

The Guardian points toward three ancient crystal doors.

**CRYSTAL GUARDIAN**

*"Prove your courage... your wisdom... and your unity."*

*"Only then shall the path open."*

The three doors slowly begin to open.

A brilliant blue light pours through them.

The heroes exchange determined looks.

Their first trial is about to begin.

---

## Ending Frame

The camera slowly pulls back.

The Crystal Guardian stands in the center of the chamber while the three glowing doors illuminate the heroes.

Fade to the next scene.
