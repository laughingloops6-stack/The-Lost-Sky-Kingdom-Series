# Scene Description

The hidden gateway rises completely.

A soft golden light pours through the opening.

Arin, Bruno, and Byte slowly walk forward.

As they cross the gateway...

The camera reveals an incredible world above the clouds.

Massive floating islands drift peacefully across the sky.

Gigantic crystal waterfalls flow into the clouds below.

Ancient crystal castles shine under the warm sunlight.

Flying creatures glide gracefully through the air.

Golden bridges connect floating mountains.

The wind carries glowing crystal petals across the sky.

The three heroes stand completely speechless.

Bruno removes his backpack and stares in amazement.

Byte records everything with excitement.

Arin quietly whispers...

**ARIN**

*"So... this is the Lost Sky Kingdom..."*

The Sky Crystal Key begins glowing brighter than ever before.

Far in the distance...

One ancient tower suddenly lights up.

Something inside the tower has sensed their arrival.
