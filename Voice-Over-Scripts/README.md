# Voice Over Scripts

## Purpose

This folder contains all official narration and dialogue scripts for **The Lost Sky Kingdom** animated series.

Every voice-over must strictly follow the Story Bible, World Bible, and Character Bible to ensure consistency across all episodes.

---

## Voice Style

- Warm
- Cinematic
- Emotional
- Family Friendly
- Inspirational
- Easy to understand

---

## Narration Rules

- Begin with a strong hook.
- Keep narration synchronized with scene timing.
- Build mystery without revealing future events.
- Maintain emotional continuity.
- End each episode with a cliffhanger.

---

## Dialogue Rules

- Natural conversations.
- Short and meaningful dialogues.
- Every character has a unique personality.
- Avoid unnecessary talking during action scenes.

---

## Script Format

Episode Number

Scene Number

Time Duration

Narration

Dialogue

Sound Effects (SFX)

Background Music (BGM)

---
