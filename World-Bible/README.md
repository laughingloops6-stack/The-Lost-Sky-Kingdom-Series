# World Bible

## The World

The story takes place in a magical world divided into two realms:

- The Surface World
- The Sky Kingdom

Thousands of years ago, both worlds lived in peace until the Sky Crystal was broken.

---

## The Surface World

The ordinary world where humans live without knowing the truth about the floating kingdom.

Features:
- Forests
- Mountains
- Ancient ruins
- Hidden caves
- Small villages

---

## The Sky Kingdom

A legendary kingdom floating above the clouds.

Features:
- Floating islands
- Crystal castles
- Flying creatures
- Ancient libraries
- Sky bridges

---

## Magic System

Magic comes from the Sky Crystal.

Types of magic:
- Wind Magic
- Light Magic
- Shadow Magic
- Crystal Magic

---

## Main Locations

- Sky Palace
- Crystal Tower
- Whisper Forest
